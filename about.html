Applied Technology Schools are model schools for the technical education, working on applying the educational and training international standards. These schools were held based on partnership between the Ministry of Education , private sector associations and the educational organizations, for the aim of advancing and levelling up the technical education system, and preparing qualified graduates for the national and international market, as well as preparing the teachers and staff according to the latest international standards through certified trainings coached by national and international experts.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Applied Technology Schools are working on preparing hundreds of students and providing them with the required skills to make them ready for the market needs.through preparing students on high educational level, having high technical skills and be able to deal with the latest technology. We achieve that by providing the students with different aspects as: an Egyptian certificate with international quality, site practical training with open vacancies for the top achievers, in addition to providing them with financial prizes during the training.


** We School is considered as one of it's kind specialized in Telecommunications and Information Technology fields, which aims at preparing qualified generation to work in the local, national and international market of highly qualified blue collar


**An academic partner (National Academy for Science and Skills NASS) with remarkable experience in the technical education field participates to manage the educational process to guarantee it’s quality.

**The school provides certified content on high level in the main specializations (Information Technology and Communication), besides the practical part in site training.
